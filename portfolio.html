Locker

Desktop and Mobile Application

In the Fall of 2016, my team and I accomplished the unimaginable. We successfully produced a Release Candidate build of “Locker” – A unified cloud storage application.

Locker is a desktop and mobile application that brings all your cloud storages (Dropbox, Google Drive, OneDrive, etc.) to one location. Leave the web browser behind and delete the traditional apps the clog up your phone. It’s just one application for all your cloud storages.

Pandora's Box

A Broken Computer is Never a Dead Computer

When it comes to computers, there are endless possibilities how to use them. For this project, I took a broken laptop and turned it into my own multi-purpose server. It currently hosts a fully responsive web page which acts as a portal to my personal media server and cloud storage - both are simultaneously hosted directly on the server.

RowdyHacks 2016

2016 was a big year for ACM - UTSA! After just a few months of preparation, we launched the first annual RowdyHacks hackathon in San Antonio. Participants had only 24 hours to create, design and develop their projects. Needless to say, it was a huge success!

Future Surface Temperatures and Precipitation Events
